About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game with roguelite elements. Fight against endless waves of monsters and see how long you can survive in this challenging world.

With each run, collect resources to upgrade your character and unlock new abilities. Choose different weapons and power-ups to create unique builds and strategies.

Game Screenshots

How to Play

  • Controls:
    • Move your character by dragging your mouse or using touch controls
    • Your character attacks automatically
    • Collect experience gems to level up
    • Choose upgrades wisely to enhance your survival chances

Features

  • Roguelite progression system
  • Multiple character types with unique abilities
  • Various weapons and power-ups to discover
  • Challenging boss fights
  • Beautiful minimalist graphics
  • Addictive gameplay that's easy to learn but hard to master

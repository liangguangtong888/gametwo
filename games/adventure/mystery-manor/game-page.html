About the Game

Enter the eerie world of Mystery Manor, where you'll explore a centuries-old haunted mansion filled with supernatural phenomena and unsolved mysteries! As a paranormal investigator, you must uncover the dark secrets hidden within the manor's walls, communicate with restless spirits, and solve the case that has baffled detectives for generations.

With atmospheric visuals, spine-tingling sound effects, and mind-bending puzzles, Mystery Manor offers a thrilling adventure that will test your detective skills and courage. Do you have what it takes to survive the night and reveal the truth behind the manor's haunted history?

Game Features

  • Explore 15 unique rooms in a sprawling Victorian mansion
  • Solve puzzles involving ghostly apparitions and supernatural clues
  • Use special equipment to detect paranormal activity
  • Collect evidence and piece together the manor's tragic history
  • Experience dynamic lighting and realistic sound effects
  • Multiple endings based on your choices and discoveries

How to Play

Control Action
WASD / Arrow Keys Move your character
Mouse Look around / Examine objects
Left Click Interact with objects / Pick up items
E Key Use ghost detection equipment
F Key Toggle flashlight
Tab Key Open journal / View collected evidence
Shift Key Run (limited stamina)

Begin your investigation in the manor's entrance hall, where you'll find your first clues. Use your ghost detection equipment to locate supernatural activity and interact with spirits. Search rooms thoroughly for hidden compartments and secret passages. Record evidence in your journal to piece together the mystery. Be careful - the manor's darker spirits may try to hinder your progress!

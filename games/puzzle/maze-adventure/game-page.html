About Maze Adventure

Maze Adventure is a challenging puzzle game where players must find their way through randomly generated mazes. As you progress, you'll need to collect keys to unlock doors while avoiding dangerous traps.

The mazes become increasingly complex with each level, adding more keys, doors, and traps to test your path-planning skills and reaction time. See how many levels you can conquer!
